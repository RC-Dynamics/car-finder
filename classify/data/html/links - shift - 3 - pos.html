Frequently Asked Questions
Are your prices negotiable?

We love data. Our pricing is driven by data as we take into account what cars within the same parameters (year, make, model, trim, mileage) would sell around the area. Our prices are always below average dealership list prices. We dont negotiate, up or down, and were very transparent about it all.

When pricing cars, our team takes into consideration the retail, auction, private party and other markets in any given region. If the price does ever drop, well be sure to notify you, but cannot guarantee that the car will still be available in the future.

Can I see multiple cars?

If you are looking to compare multiple cars in Shifts inventory, we invite you to come to our parking hub for your appointment so that you can see them both at the same time. To schedule one of these appointments, please call us at 415-969-2679.

Is this car still available?

Our website is updated live. As long as the car is not listed as Coming Soon, Sale Pending, or Sold it is available for test drives.

Does Shift provide warranties?

Every Shift Certified car comes with a 30-day limited warranty and a 5-day return guarantee. In addition to the included warranty, Shift offers vehicle protection plans that safeguard you from the unexpected costs of car ownership.
