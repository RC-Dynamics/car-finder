See the full Shift inspection process

We want to know everything about every car we list. To do this, each car undergoes a thorough inspection process, from initial appraisal to a mechanical deep dive into all systems and components of the vehicle.

Initial inspection app

Our Concierges are equipped with proprietary technology to record any and all findings from the very first time they see a car. Interviewing the seller and recording the history of the car allows us to get a jump start on any issues that might need to be addressed before the car is listed for sale. We also require all sellers using the site to be transparent about any imperfections in their cars.

Exhaustive mechanic checklist

Certified, licensed mechanics dig deeper into the car to inspect the machine itself. They make sure that everything is operating as it should be, and make any replacements or tune-ups so the car is as good as new when it gets into a buyer’s hands. We share the results of this inspection with all buyers on the following criteria:

Engine

  • Noise
  • Motor mounts
  • Exhaust leak
  • Oil level/condition
  • Oil leaks/seals/gaskets
  • Turbo/supercharger
  • Smoke
  • Driveability

Electrical system

  • Alternator/regulator
  • Starter
  • Power window/seat motor
  • Wiper motor
  • Power locks
  • Power roof motor/antenna
  • All manual switches
  • Cruise control
  • Speedometer/odometer
  • Instrument panel gauges
  • Horn

Drive axle

  • Vibration
  • Noise
  • Fluid level/leaks/seals/gaskets
  • CV/U joints/drive shafts
  • CV boots
  • Locking hubs

Steering system

  • Fluid levels/leaks
  • Pump/gear
  • Lines/hoses
  • Linkages/tie rods
  • Shaft/couplings
  • Tilt wheel

Brake system

  • Fluid level/leaks
  • Master cylinder
  • Pump/booster
  • Discs/pads % remaining
  • Wheel cylinders/drums
  • Lines & fittings

Transmission

  • Fluid level/condition
  • Shift points (automatic)
  • Slipping (automatic)
  • Noise
  • Transmission mounts
  • Fluid leaks/seals/gaskets
  • Shift linkage

Cooling & fuel system

  • Water pump
  • Engine fan motor
  • Fan & clutch
  • Radiator/leaks
  • Coolant level/condition
  • Gaskets/hoses
  • Fuel tank/lines/injectors
  • Fuel/injection pump

Suspension system

  • Control arms
  • Ball joints/king pins
  • Wheel bearings
  • Torsion bars/springs
  • Stabilizer shaft & links
  • Struts

Air conditioning

  • Blower motor
  • Compressor/clutch
  • Condenser
  • Evaporator
  • Idler pulley

Vehicle modifications

  • Tire size/% remaining
  • Suspension/engine
  • Emissions equipment
